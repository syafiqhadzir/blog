## The Evolution of the Tester

- **2022**: "I write Cypress scripts."
- **2023**: "I prompt LLMs to write Cypress scripts."
- **2024**: "I audit the agents that generate the code."
- **2025**: "I am the Architect of Trust."

We moved from **Verification** (Did we build the thing right?) to **Validation** (Did we build the right thing?) to **Ethics** (Should we have built this thing at all?). That last question is the one that keeps me up at nightâ€”and it should keep you up too.
